Abstract

The purpose of this ontology is to provide a common vocabulary for representing the main entities and data of public and private parking facilities in a municipality. It includes the representation of parking spaces both on and off public roads. Its scope is limited to data that can be used for mobility management purposes, such as vehicle access to parking facilities, occupancy levels, spaces by permit type (e.g., residents), and spaces by vehicle type, which are part of the standard functions of local authorities.
